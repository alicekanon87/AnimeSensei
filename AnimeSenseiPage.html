About

During summer vacation, I found myself binge-watching anime on Netflix... until I was completely lost by the overwhelming number of choices. And I thought: "If only someone could help me find exactly what I would love..."

That's how AnimeSensei was born: a personal anime teacher that understands your tastes. Our algorithm analyzes what you like (and what you don't) to provide recommendations perfectly tailored to your profile.

Using data from MyAnimeList and a data-driven approach, we want to help all anime enthusiasts find their next gem!
